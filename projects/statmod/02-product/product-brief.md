# Product Brief

## Thesis

`STAT Mod` should be the central progression brain for a combat-heavy Minecraft modpack, with stats driving combat, survival, perk, stamina, and magic systems across connected mods.

## Current Product Direction

- keep one strong progression authority
- integrate external combat or magic mods through STAT Mod gates instead of parallel progression systems
- favor native upstream UX when it reduces maintenance cost

## Active Design Tracks

- Epic Fight combat and stamina alignment
- Puffish-driven perk and skill tree presentation
- race-aware magic identity
- Iron's Spellbooks as the current preferred spell runtime
