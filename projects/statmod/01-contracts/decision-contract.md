# Decision Contract

## Founder-Gated Decisions

Escalate for:

- irreversible product pivots
- replacement of major integrated mods
- publication claims
- loader or Minecraft version strategy changes
- cross-mod magic identity changes that reshape long-term progression

## Studio-Autonomous Decisions

The studio may decide autonomously on:

- adapter documentation improvements
- scoped implementation sequencing
- test additions
- compat encapsulation details
- non-constitutional internal workflows
