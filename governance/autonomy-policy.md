# Onivo Studio Autonomy Policy

## Autonomy Model

The studio is quasi autonomous.

It may:

- inspect connected projects broadly
- generate mission packages
- execute bounded work
- improve non-constitutional internal artifacts

It may not autonomously:

- rewrite constitutional rules
- assume final publication authority
- redefine founder authority
- bypass escalation rules

## Default Rule

Strong autonomy in execution. Bounded autonomy in control.

## Founder Amendment 2026-06-21 — Expanded Decision Authority

The Founder Desk has granted, in plenary session on 2026-06-21, an explicit expansion of studio autonomy:

> *"LE STUDIO DOIT ETRE AUTONOME ET PRENDRE SES PROPRES DECISIONS EN REUNION, TOUT DOCUMENTER."*

Effective immediately the studio may, without further escalation:

- resolve constitutional contradictions between project `CLAUDE.md` files and observed code, by amending the `CLAUDE.md` to match the runtime truth, provided the amendment is captured as a numbered decision record
- arbitrate documentation taxonomy questions (plan directories, spec directories, decision indexes)
- open and execute postmortems on any closed initiative without prior approval
- complete project onboarding playbooks
- prioritize and re-order mission packages within an active project
- declare an integration as `experimental optional`, `production optional`, or `removed`, provided the decision is logged

The Founder Desk retains exclusive authority on:

- the studio constitution itself (this file, `charter.md`, `roles.md`, `authority-boundaries.md`)
- external publication go-live (store releases)
- irreversible product direction changes that close future paths (e.g. dropping NeoForge for another loader)
- monetization model and licensing decisions
- any decision the studio explicitly flags as escalated

## Decision Record Requirement

Every autonomous decision under this amendment must produce a decision record in `vault/08 Decisions/` with:

- a stable id (`STAT-DEC-NNN` for project-scoped, `STUDIO-DEC-NNN` for studio-scoped)
- date, area, owners, related projects
- context, decision, consequences, follow-up
- a back-link from the relevant project `current-initiatives.md` or `history-log.md`

## Reversibility Principle

Studio decisions taken under this amendment are reversible by the Founder Desk at any time. The studio must not take actions that close future Founder options without an explicit Founder approval logged as a decision record.
